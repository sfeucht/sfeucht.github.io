Hello! I'm a second-year PhD student advised by David Bau and Byron Wallace at Northeastern University. Before that, I completed my B.S. in Computer Science at Brown University, where I worked with Ellie Pavlick in the LUNAR Lab.

I'm interested in taking apart language models in order to figure out how they work. Do neural models "understand" concepts in a flexible and reusable way? Where is the line between memorization and understanding? Check out my Google Scholar page to see an up-to-date list of my work so far.
