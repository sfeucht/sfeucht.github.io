writing that’s not language (yet)

An interactive essay I worked on for Rereading Writing (LITR1231E) in Summer 2021 with Prof. John Cayley. Explores how humans ascribe meaning to text generated by machines, and how state-of-the-art NLP models push the boundaries of what we can comfortably call language.

two-sentence horror story generator

A simple BART model fine-tuned to generate two-sentence horror stories. It also has a Twitter page (which is currently not active)!

surreal spaces (a series)

Selections from my concentration for my A.P. Studio Art portfolio. I was interested in creating somewhat dreamlike illustrations of different settings, drawing from my own personal experiences.
